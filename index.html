Our Firm

Dorward Engineering Services Ltd. specializes in planning, design, studies, assessments, contract services and construction supervision, project management, fire protection, life safety and security, commissioning, technical writing and other electrical engineering services for commercial, residential, industrial, institutional and historical building and projects.

Our History

Dorward Engineering Services Ltd. was established by Ross Dorward, P. Eng in 1992 as a second generation firm to continue the electrical engineering function of Dorward Enterprises Ltd., which provided electrical engineering services in the Yukon Territory from 1981 under the direction of Fred Dorward, P. Eng. Ross Dorward has accumulated more than 40 years experience in the electrical industry, both domestically and abroad.
